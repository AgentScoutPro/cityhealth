Top Priority Issues
No LocalBusiness / MedicalClinic schema on location pages
Critical ▼

Yoast outputs generic Organization schema sitewide — no address, phone, hours, or coordinates. Location pages need MedicalClinic + LocalBusiness schema to appear in Google's local pack and Knowledge Panel.

Invisible to local pack features. Google cannot confirm your NAP from structured data.

Raw HTML source on both location pages — schema only contains "@type": "Organization"

Fix

See the Schema tab for ready-to-paste JSON-LD blocks for both locations. Add via Yoast's custom schema field or a Custom HTML block.

Location URL slugs spell "Mesa" as "Meza"
Critical ▼

/locations/longmore-road-meza-az/
/locations/power-road-meza-az/

"Mesa AZ" is the primary local keyword. The misspelling means the URL doesn't carry that keyword signal. The canonical tag also locks in the wrong URL.

Create new pages at correct slugs → 301 redirect old URLs → update internal links + sitemap.

Viewport disables user zoom (accessibility + mobile usability)
High ▼

<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1, maximum-scale=1, user-scalable=0">

Google flags this as a Mobile Usability error in Search Console. Also an ADA accessibility violation.

Edit the Salient theme viewport setting. Remove user-scalable=0 and change maximum-scale=1 to maximum-scale=5.

Content & E-E-A-T

3 issues found
About page undersells credentials for a medical practice
High ▼

Staff listed with minimal detail:
• Brad Forbes (CEO) — listed as DC, no bio or education
• Dr. Ana Garcia — listed only as "Bilingual - Spanish" (no specialty mentioned)
• Terri Caruso (AGACNP-C) — credential shown, no bio
• No years in business, no awards, no accreditations

Google's E-E-A-T guidelines weight medical sites heavily on expertise and trustworthiness. Thin about pages with minimal credentials directly affect rankings for health topics (YMYL — Your Money Your Life).

Fix

Add individual provider bio pages with: specialty, medical education, years of experience, conditions treated, photo. Add Person + Physician schema. Mention years City Health has been operating.

59 blog posts with bulk-updated dates suggest mass AI refresh
Medium ▼

48 of 59 posts share exact lastmod date of 2025-09-18 — a mass update, not individual editorial improvements.

Scaled content refreshes without substantive improvements can trigger Google's scaled content abuse policies.

Identify top 10–15 posts by traffic in Search Console. Do real substantive updates: add original data, patient-facing FAQs, internal links to service pages, author attribution with schema. Don't just touch dates.

Legal entity name discrepancy on terms page
Medium ▼

Terms page refers to "Fiesta Health Services LLC d/b/a City Health Services" — the former name appears nowhere else on the site.

Brand consistency is a trust signal. If the company has rebranded, all references to the old name should be cleaned up.
